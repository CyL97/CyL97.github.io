Chaoyu Li

Master Student

University of Southern California

I am a 2nd-year master student in Applied Data Science at the University of Southern California, advised by Prof. Ram Nevatia. Before that, I got my B.S. degree from Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

My research interests mainly lie in computer vision, especially in:

  • * Object and Action Recognition: Object detection in complex environments; Action recognition in distorted or adversarial samples.
  • * Human Action Analysis: Recognize human body or hand action; 3D modeling and rendering of human faces or bodies.
  • * Learning from limited supervision: Complete unsupervised or semi-supervised adversarial training with little data.
